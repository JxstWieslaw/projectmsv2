About

A Technical Solution To The Issue At Bologna Support Foundation

  • mid August 2021

    Humble Beginnings

    Bologna Support Foundatoon, a charity organisation, has become large enough that their unorganised projects management can no longer suffice operations. They relied heavily on casually note taking and traditional means to store and maintain data. There is urgent need for a way to task and allocate projects to employees whilst keeping track of progress of the respective projects.

  • September 2021

    Hence they approached CodeVilla

    We carried out a comprehensive feasibility study in the form of Interviews and Questionnaires. We used the Agile Development Technique so as to ascertain quick version releases and easier scalabilty of features on top of each other.

  • October 2021

    Transition to Full Service (v1)

    After thorough Unit and Integration Testing, version 1.0. has been released.Validation, Authorisation and Authentication throughout the app has been ensured. The Minimum Viable Product has been deployed and is now in use. Beta testing is an ongoing phase so as to detect errors and bugs earlier in development.

  • Novemeber 2021:

    Phase Two Expansion

    From version 1.0.0, we can expect :

    ▪️ Create, Delete , Update the Record employees and projects.

    ▪️ Track Workloads of projects allocated to each employee.

    ▪️ Track Progress of projects (completed, in progress or not started)

    ▪️ Different Access levels between an Admin User and Standard User.

    A major release was expected. Version 1.0.0 was a proof of concept hence it is highly scalable. UI and other front-end upgrades should be expected. Introduction of more modules should be expected. Steadily, it will be an efficient and effective solution to the issue at Bologna Support Foundation.

  • December 2021

    Transition to Full Service (v2)

    From Version 2:

    ▪️ Workflow has been increased due to optimisations.

    ▪️ Reports and Charts to show analysis of how everything goes

    ▪️ A Dashboard that will assist in keeping current positions of the company.

    ▪️ A Revamped User Interface with a variety of features

    A full solution to their problem has been implemented. We await version 3 anticipated in February 2022
